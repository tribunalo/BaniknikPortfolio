Hey there! I'm Nik, a third-year BSIT student. I consider myself a simple, boyish girl who cherishes life with boundless happiness. As a loving and caring daughter, I embody traits like being gleeful, soft-hearted, trustworthy, friendly, and hardworking. Beyond these, I nurture a bigger dream and strive each day to become the best version of myself.

Life's journey is challenging, but I'm resolute in facing it head-on, fueled by the unwavering support of those beside me through both highs and lows. I pour my heart into everything I do, for life must go on.

One of my passions is singing. While I may lack the confidence to perform in front of a crowd, the joy it brings me is undeniable. I sing primarily for myself, finding solace and satisfaction in the melodies.

Dancing is another love of mine. Despite the challenges of performing in front of an audience, it provides a unique sense of happiness and freedom, allowing me to escape from life's problems and struggles.

I'm also a sporty individual, adept at playing volleyball, football, badminton, and more. My diverse interests keep life vibrant and exciting.
